Homemade Biscuits

Description:

These biscuits are so deliciously soft and buttery. They are easy to make, easy to knead, and a nice, warm treat!

This recipe belongs to Sugar Spun Run. They're pretty good, but they're just biscuits.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 0.75 whole milk

    • Steps:

    1. Chill your butter in the freezer for 10-20 minutes.
    2. Preheat oven to 425F and line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
    3. Combine flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt. Mix well.
    4. Shred frozen butter into the flour mixture and stir.
    5. Add milk and stir until combined.
    6. Transfer the dough to a well-floured surface. Use your hands to work the dough together.
    7. Once the dough is cohesive, fold in half over itself and gently flatten.
    8. Rotate the dough 90 degrees and fold in half again. Repeat this step 5-6 times.
    9. Use your hands to gently flatten the dough to 1" thick. Lightly dust a biscuit cutter and make close cuts to get at least 6 biscuits.
    10. Bake for 12 minutes or until tops are beginning to turn lightly golden brown.
    11. If desired, brush with melted, salted butter after removing from the oven.
